Included Features

Lead Form Integration

Every quiz completion captures the lead's email, profile, score, temperature, and individual question answers. Data is stored in Supabase with full export capability.

Integrate with your existing CRM, email marketing platform, or sales pipeline. Webhooks available for real-time lead notifications.

Included

Custom Domain

The quiz funnel deploys to your own domain or subdomain (e.g., quiz.uphillathlete.com). Full SSL, fast global delivery via Vercel edge network.

Branded experience from first click to email delivery. No third-party quiz platform branding visible to visitors.

Included

Lead Intelligence Agent

Built-in analytics dashboard at /admin tracks every funnel metric: page views, quiz starts, completion rates, email captures, temperature distribution, answer analysis, and UTM sources.

Password-protected. Chart.js visualizations. Real-time data. Identifies which traffic sources produce the highest-quality leads and which quiz answers predict conversion.

Optional Add-Ons

Growth Add-Ons

IG DM Automation

Automated Instagram DM sequences triggered by quiz completion or specific keywords in comments and messages. Deliver quiz results, training tips, and coaching invitations directly in Instagram DMs.

Increases engagement with leads who prefer social messaging over email. Complements the email sequences with a second touchpoint channel.

$99 /month

Voice Follow-Up

AI-powered voice calls to hot leads within 5 minutes of quiz completion. Personalized script references their profile, score, and answers. Routes to live agent for coaching consultation booking.

Hot leads contacted within 5 minutes convert at 10x the rate of leads contacted after 24 hours. Voice follow-up captures the peak moment of engagement.

$1,000 /month

Text Message Sequences

SMS follow-up sequences triggered by quiz completion. 3-5 text messages over 7 days with training tips and product links. Complements email for leads who prefer mobile messaging.

SMS open rates average 98% vs. 20-30% for email. Reaches leads who may not check email regularly but always have their phone.

$49 /month

Additional Quiz Funnels

Build additional quiz funnels targeting different segments or objectives. Examples: "What's Your Expedition Readiness Score?", "Trail Running Training Assessment", "Mountain Strength Self-Test."

Multiple quiz funnels segment your audience more precisely and provide fresh lead capture points for returning visitors who have already completed the primary quiz.

$2,000 each
