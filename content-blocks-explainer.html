Uphill Athlete - Mountain Training Profile

How 145 content blocks create thousands of unique email combinations. Every lead receives emails tailored to their profile and their specific quiz answers.

1 What Are Content Blocks?

Content blocks are personalized paragraphs and sentences that get injected into email templates based on who the lead is and how they answered the quiz. Instead of writing separate emails for every possible combination, the system uses 26 email templates with placeholder variables that get swapped out at send time.

There are two types of content blocks:

5 How It Works

Content blocks are stored as rows in a CSV file, seeded into a database table, and resolved at email send time. Here is the process:

1

Content Blocks CSV

145 rows of personalized content. Each row maps to one email + one profile or one answer. The CSV has 5 columns: Email ID, Block Type, Block Key, Block Value, and Content.

↓
2

Database Seeding

During setup, the CSV is parsed and every row is inserted into the content_blocks database table. This happens once during deployment -- the same command that creates the quiz tables.

↓
3

Email Scheduling

When a lead completes the quiz, the system schedules their email sequence based on their temperature score (hot/warm/cold). Each scheduled email references a template ID like "W-1" or "CN-4."

↓
4

Content Block Resolution

When it is time to send an email, the system looks up the template, then queries the content blocks table for matching blocks. It finds the right profile block (based on the lead's profile ID) and the right answer callbacks (based on their Q1 and Q5 answers).

↓
5

Variable Replacement

The system replaces {{profile_block}}, {{answer_callback_1}}, and {{answer_callback_2}} in the email body with the matched content. If no block is found for a variable, it is replaced with an empty string (no error, the email still sends).

↓
6

Personalized Email Sent

The lead receives an email that reads as if it were written specifically for them. A Summit Chaser mountaineer stuck at a plateau gets different content than an Alpine Apprentice who does not know where to start.

Graceful fallbacks: If a content block is missing (for example, if a new profile is added but blocks have not been written yet), the placeholder is silently removed. The email still sends with the generic template content intact. No errors, no broken emails.
