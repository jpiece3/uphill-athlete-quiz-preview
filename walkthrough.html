Quiz Funnel Walkthrough

What's Your Mountain Training Profile?

A complete guide to the Uphill Athlete quiz funnel: how it works, why it works, and what it delivers to your business.

Section 1

What This Is

The Mountain Training Profile quiz is a lead generation funnel built specifically for Uphill Athlete. It captures email addresses by offering genuine training value in exchange: a personalized mountain athlete profile, training stage assessment, and actionable next steps.

The quiz identifies which of five mountain athlete profiles best matches each visitor, measures their training readiness on a 0-100 scale, and routes them to the most relevant Uphill Athlete products and resources. The entire experience takes approximately 2 minutes.

Behind the scenes, the funnel segments leads into hot, warm, and cold categories and enrolls them in temperature-specific email sequences with layered personalization based on their profile and quiz answers.

Section 2

How It Works

1

Landing Page

The visitor arrives via organic search, social media, paid ads, or existing Uphill Athlete traffic. The landing page presents the quiz value proposition: "Discover Your Mountain Training Profile." A single CTA drives quiz engagement.

2

7-Question Quiz

Seven questions across seven different input types (card selection, scale slider, tag cloud, emoji scale, multiple choice, yes/no toggle, star rating). Each answer contributes to the 0-100 readiness score and generates tags for profile matching. Questions are mountain-training specific, not generic fitness.

3

Email Capture

After all 7 questions, a screen invites the visitor to enter their email to receive their personalized training profile. The copy references the specific value being delivered: "Get your personalized Mountain Training Profile, training stage assessment, and actionable next steps."

4

Loading Experience

A branded loading sequence builds anticipation with mountain-training-specific messages: "Evaluating your aerobic base...", "Mapping your training trajectory...", "Identifying your next milestone...", "Building your personalized training roadmap..."

5

Results Page (Pathway Archetype)

The results page uses a milestone map visualization showing the athlete's position on the training journey from Foundation to Peak Readiness. It includes: profile name and headline, key training insight, strength card, growth card, three actionable next steps, and temperature-appropriate CTAs linking to Uphill Athlete products.

6

Email Sequences

26 emails across 5 sequences deliver ongoing value. All leads get 3 Welcome emails. Then they branch into Cold Nurture (7), Warm Activation (6), or Hot Path (4) based on score. Finally, 3 Re-Engagement emails follow. Emails include profile-specific and answer-specific content blocks for deep personalization.

Section 3

Why It Works

Identity-based engagement. Mountain athletes are drawn to discover their "type." The five profiles (Summit Chaser, Trail Blazer, Alpine Apprentice, Mountain Multisport, Comeback Climber) map to real athlete identities in the Uphill Athlete community. Once someone claims their profile, product recommendations feel natural, not promotional.

Value before the ask. The email capture happens after all 7 questions, after the visitor has invested 90+ seconds in the quiz. The results deliver genuine training insights and actionable advice. The email sequences continue providing educational value for 2-3 weeks before any conversion-focused messaging.

Evidence-based credibility. Uphill Athlete's core differentiator is 100+ years of endurance science adapted for mountain sports. The quiz and email content consistently reference this methodology, reinforcing the brand's authority positioning.

Layered personalization. With 5 profiles, 3 temperature bands, discipline-specific callbacks, and challenge-specific callbacks, the funnel generates thousands of unique content combinations from 26 email templates. Every lead receives messaging that feels tailored to them.

Industry benchmarks: Quiz funnels typically convert 30-50% of visitors to leads (vs. 2-5% for static landing pages). Temperature-segmented email sequences see 15-25% higher open rates than generic sequences. Personalized content blocks increase click-through rates by 20-35%.
Section 4

Where to Use It

Website Traffic

Add to uphillathlete.com navigation, article sidebars, and exit-intent popups. Captures existing traffic that is not ready to purchase.

Organic Social

Share quiz link on Instagram bio, Instagram stories, YouTube descriptions, and Facebook posts. "What kind of mountain athlete are you?" drives curiosity clicks.

Paid Advertising

Facebook and Instagram ads targeting mountain sports interests. Google Ads on "mountaineering training plan" and "trail running training" keywords.

Email Marketing

Send to existing email list as a re-engagement tool. "We built something for you" framing. Captures profile data for subscribers who have never been segmented.

Podcast Promotion

"Voices of Experience" podcast listeners are already warm. Quiz link in show notes and verbal CTA during episodes.

Partner Distribution

Share with expedition companies, trail race organizers, and climbing gyms as a value-add for their audiences. Co-branded versions available.
