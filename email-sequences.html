W-2 Day 2 The one training rule every mountain athlete should follow Welcome

Hi {{first_name}},

There is one principle that separates effective mountain training from wasted effort. It has been proven over 100 years of endurance science.

The 80/20 Rule: 80% of your training volume should be at or below your aerobic threshold. The other 20% is where you push.

Most mountain athletes get this backward. They train too hard on easy days and too easy on hard days.
