Strategy Pack

Social Content Calendar

4-week content plan with 3 pillars, platform strategy, and specific post ideas to drive quiz funnel traffic.

Foundation

Content Pillars

1. Training Science

Evidence-based training principles. Aerobic base, intensity distribution, periodization, training zones. Educational content that positions Uphill Athlete as the authority.

mountaineering training aerobic base training zones periodization

2. Athlete Stories

Real results from real athletes. Summit successes, race finishes, comeback stories. Social proof that builds trust and drives emotional connection.

athlete transformation summit success coaching results

3. Mountain Culture

The mountain athlete lifestyle. Gear, destinations, philosophy. Community belonging content that reinforces the Uphill Athlete identity and brand values.

mountain sports trail running expedition prep

4. Quiz Promotion

Direct quiz funnel promotion. Profile reveals, quiz teasers, result previews. Drives quiz traffic through curiosity and identity-based hooks.

mountain training profile training assessment athlete type

Platform Strategy

Instagram

  • Feed: Mountain photography with training tip overlays. Carousel education posts. Athlete story highlights. 3-4 posts per week.
  • Stories: Daily training tips, poll stickers ("What's your biggest training challenge?"), quiz link in bio CTA. Quiz teaser carousels.
  • Reels: 15-30 second training tips. "One thing most mountain athletes get wrong" format. Coach quick takes.
  • Bio link: Quiz landing page URL

LinkedIn

  • Posts: Professional athlete angle. "Corporate athletes" who balance demanding careers with mountain objectives. 2 posts per week.
  • Articles: Long-form training methodology pieces. Periodization frameworks for busy professionals. 1 per month.
  • Audience: 35-55 professionals, high income, mountain sport hobbyists. Key demographic for coaching conversion.

YouTube

  • Long-form: Training methodology deep dives. Athlete transformation stories. Coach Q&A sessions. 1-2 per month.
  • Shorts: Training tips, gear quick takes, mountain motivation. Cross-post from Reels. 2-3 per week.
  • Description links: Quiz URL in every video description
